Does surgery help obesity?

Substance Definition
surgery: "the branch of medical practice that treats injuries, diseases, and deformities by the physical removal, repair, or readjustment of organs and tissues, often involving cutting into the body"
source: https://www.lexico.com/en/definition/surgery

Condition Definition
obesity: "obesity is a complex disease involving an excessive amount of body fat. It is a medical problem that increases your risk of other diseases and health problems, such as heart disease, diabetes, high blood pressure and certain cancers"
source: https://www.mayoclinic.org/diseases-conditions/obesity/symptoms-causes/syc-20375742
