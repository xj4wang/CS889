Do insoles help back pain?

Substance Definition
insoles: "a thin piece of material that you put at the bottom of the inside of a shoe for comfort"
source: Merriam-Webster - http://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/insoles

Condition Definition
back pain: The spine is a column of bones (vertebrae) held together by muscles, tendons and ligaments and cushioned by shock-absorbing disks. A problem in any part of your spine can cause back pain.
source: http://www.mayoclinic.org/symptoms/back-pain/basics/definition/sym-20050878
